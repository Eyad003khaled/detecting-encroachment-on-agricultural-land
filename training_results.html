Interpretation & Next Steps

🔍 What the model learned

Top signal: MNDWI_diff_mean (water content drop) followed by BSI and NDWI variability. Counter-intuitively, water-related indices dominate over NDVI/NDBI — likely because agricultural land in KEMET1 includes irrigated plots whose water signature changes when encroachment clears vegetation.

⚠️ Overfitting diagnosis

Train AUC=1.0 vs Test AUC=0.74 is a clear overfit signal. Root cause: only 22 positive examples in 135 training pairs. The RF memorises the training positives. Fix: add more positive tiles, apply stricter max_depth, or switch to cross-validated training.

✅ Recommended next steps

1. Use threshold=0.16 for screening (100% recall) then hand-verify flagged tiles. 2. Tune max_depth=8–12 and min_samples_leaf to reduce overfit. 3. Add SMOTE or augment with synthetic positive pairs. 4. Consider per-tile cross-validation given limited sample size.
